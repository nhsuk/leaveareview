Prescriptions and delivery

This pharmacy offers:

  • NHS one-off prescriptions
  • NHS repeat prescriptions
  • delivery service
  • online ordering of medicines and prescriptions
  • reminders when your repeat prescriptions are due
  • supply of stoma bags and other appliances
  • stoma bag fitting
  • advice about appliances such as a stoma bag, catheter or wound drainage pouch

Repeat prescriptions

You can choose this pharmacy to have your repeat prescriptions sent to them by your GP surgery. This means that you will not have to visit the surgery every time you need a repeat prescription.

This pharmacy will be able to order repeat prescriptions on your behalf from your GP surgery.
