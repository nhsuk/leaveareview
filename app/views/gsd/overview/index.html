Description

Leeds Mind is an independent charity and we are very proud to be affiliated with the Mind group. This includes over 120 local Mind associations, and many Mind shops. We work closely with other Local Mind Associations throughout Yorkshire and the Humber, to ensure we are delivering the best service possible.
