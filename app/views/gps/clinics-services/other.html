Other clinic and services

This GP surgery is part of a wide group practice – a network of local GP surgeries. You can may be able to use these clinics and services at another GP surgery. For further information contact reception on 012345 67089 or visit the surgery website.

  • Mental health therapy
  • Self-referral mental health counselling
  • Social prescribing – this refers to people to community activities and classes to improve wellbeing

Minor surgery
