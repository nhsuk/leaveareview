Urgent appointments with a GP

If you need medical help or advice within the next 24 hours and if you have a medical need this GP surgery offers:

  • 24-hour same day appointment – phone reception at 8.30am to book
  • telephone appointments – non-bookable, phone reception at 12pm
  • evening appointments on Wednesday 6 to 8pm – phone or visit reception to book

Routine appointments with a GP

To book these:

  • visit reception
  • phone reception - 01432 263063
  • Book online. You need to be registered with your GP’s online service to do this

Out of hours GP appointments

If you need to see a GP outside surgery opening hours you can book an evening or weekend appointment with a neighbouring GP surgery.

To book an out of hours appointment, phone reception (or phone 111 if this surgery is closed).

Appointments are also available with a neighbouring GP surgery on bank holidays.

Routine appointments with other healthcare staff

  • nurse appointments are available for vaccinations, blood pressure checks, health reviews, wound management, cervical screening, smoking cessation and lifestyle advice
  • physiotherapist appointments are available for back, neck and joint pain
  • mental health therapist appointments are available for stress, anxiety or depression

To book these:

  • visit the reception desk
  • phone reception
