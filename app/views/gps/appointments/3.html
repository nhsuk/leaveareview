Urgent appointments with a GP

If you need to speak/see a GP within the next 48 hours and if you have a medical need this GP surgery offers:

  • 24-hour same day appointment
  • telephone appointments
  • evening and weekend appointments
To book these:
  • phone reception
  • phone 111 (if reception is closed)

For evening and weekend appointments, you may be given an appointment at a neighbouring surgery. Appointments at a neighbouring surgery are also available on bank holidays.

Routine appointments with a GP

To book these:
  • visit the reception desk
  • phone reception
  • Book online

Routine appointments with other healthcare staff

  • nurse appointments are available for vaccinations, blood pressure checks, health reviews, wound management, cervical screening, smoking cessation and lifestyle advice
  • physiotherapist appointments are available for back, neck and joint pain
  • mental health therapist appointments are available for stress, anxiety or depression
