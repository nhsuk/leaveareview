Appointment types

Same day appointment with a GP

Appointments are booked on a first come first served basis.

How to book
  • phone the surgery at 8.30am - 01432 263063

Routine appointment with a GP of your choice

You can book a routine appointment up to 4 weeks in advance.

How to book
  • visit the surgery
  • phone the surgery - 01432 263063
  • book online

Same day telephone consultations with a GP

These appointments are available 12 to 1pm daily on a first come first served basis.

How to book
  • visit the surgery
  • phone the surgery - 01432 263063
  • book online

Appointments with other health professionals at this surgery

Appointments are available with a nurse, physiotherapist, mental health therapist. You do not need a GP referral to book these appointments.

How to book
  • visit the surgery
  • phone the surgery - 01432 263063
  • book online

Out of hours GP appointments at another surgery

If you need to see a GP outside surgery opening hours you can book an evening or weekend appointment with a neighbouring GP surgery.

How to book
  • phone the surgery - 01432 263063
  • phone 111 if this surgery is closed

Appointments are also available with a neighbouring GP surgery on bank holidays.
