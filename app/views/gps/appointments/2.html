Routine GP appointment

Book a routine appointment with a GP of your choice up to 4 weeks in advance. You can Book online, phone reception on - 01432 263063 or visit the surgery.

Same day GP appointment

Appointments are booked on a first come first served basis. You can book these by phoning reception at 8.30am - 01432 263063

Same day GP telephone appointment

These appointments are available 12 to 1pm daily on a first come first served basis. You can Book online, phone reception on - 01432 263063 or visit the surgery.

Appointments with other health professionals at this surgery

Appointments are available with a nurse, physiotherapist, mental health therapist. You do not need a GP referral to book these appointments. You can Book online, phone reception on - 01432 263063 or visit the surgery.

Out of hours GP appointments at another surgery

If you need to see a GP outside surgery opening hours you can book an evening or weekend appointment with a neighbouring GP surgery. You can book these by phoning reception on - 01432 263063 or by calling 111 if the surgery is closed.

Appointments are also available with a neighbouring GP surgery on bank holidays.

Visit the surgery website for further information about booking an appointment.
