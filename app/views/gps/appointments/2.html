Appointment types

Same day appointment with a GP

How to book: phone the surgery at 8.30am - 01432 263063. Appointments are booked on a first come first served basis.

Routine appointment with a GP of your choice

How to book: phone or visit the surgery or book online. You need to be registered with your GPâ€™s online service to do this.

Same day telephone consultations with a GP

How to book: phone or visit the surgery. These appointments are available 12 to 1pm daily on a first come first served basis.

Appointments with other health professionals at this surgery

Appointments are available with a:

  • nurse
  • physiotherapist
  • mental health therapist

How to book: phone or visit reception. You do not need a GP referral to book these appointments.

Out of hours GP appointments

If you need to see a GP outside surgery opening hours you can book an evening or weekend appointment with a neighbouring GP surgery.

How to book: phone reception (or phone 111 if this surgery is closed).

Appointments are also available with a neighbouring GP surgery on bank holidays.
