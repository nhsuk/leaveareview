Out of hours GP appointments at another surgery

If you need to see a GP outside surgery opening hours you can book an evening or weekend appointment with a neighbouring GP surgery. You can book these by phoning the surgery or by calling 111 if the surgery is closed.

Appointments are also available with a neighbouring GP surgery on bank holidays.
