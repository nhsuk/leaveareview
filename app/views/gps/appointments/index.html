How to book an appointment

  • Phone reception - 01432 263063
  • Book online – you need to be registered with your GP’s online service to do this. Routine appointment are always available to book online

Visit the surgery website for further information about booking an appointment.

Appointments at this surgery

GP appointments

This surgery offers a range of GP appointments:

  • routine appointments (with a GP of your choice)
  • same day/48 hour appointments (with a rota GP)
  • drop-in appointments (on the 1st Saturday of the month)
  • telephone consultations
  • evening appointments (every Wednesday 6 to 8pm)

Appointments with a nurse

Nurse appointments are available for:

  • vaccinations
  • blood pressure checks
  • health reviews
  • wound management
  • cervical screening
  • stop smoking and other lifestyle advice

Appointments with other healthcare staff

Appointments are available with a:

  • physiotherapist – for back, neck and joint pain
  • mental health therapist – for stress, anxiety or depression

You do not need a GP referral to book these appointments.

Out of hours GP appointments

If you need to see a GP outside surgery opening hours you can book an evening or weekend appointment with a neighbouring GP surgery.

To book an out of hours appointment, phone reception (or phone 111 if this surgery is closed).

Appointments are also available with a neighbouring GP surgery on bank holidays.
