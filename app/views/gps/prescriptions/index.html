How to order a repeat prescription

You do not need to see a GP to order a repeat prescription. You can order a repeat prescription from the surgery in several ways:

  • by post - enclosing a stamped addressed envelope if you want the prescription to be returned to you
  • by phone - 01432 263063 between 10am to 1pm
  • in person at reception

Check with your local pharmacist as they may be able to order a repeat prescription on your behalf.

This service is for medicine that you take regularly. It is not for one-off prescriptions, these will require an appointment with a GP.

Electronic prescriptions

This is a new way to get your medicines and appliances.

It means you can save time by not having to pick up your paper prescription from the surgery. Instead your GP will send it electronically to the place you choose.

When you need more medicine, you can reorder it directly from the from the pharmacy.

Find out more about electronic prescriptions

Order your repeat prescriptions online

You can order your repeat prescriptions online. If you do this, you can choose which pharmacy they are sent to.

You must be registered with your online GP service to do this.

Find out more about using online services
