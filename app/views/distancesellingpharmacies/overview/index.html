Distance selling pharmacy

Prescriptions and delivery

This distance selling pharmacy offers:

  • NHS one-off prescriptions
  • NHS repeat prescriptions
  • Online and/or telephone ordering
  • Nationwide delivery
  • Prescription delivery to GP practices
