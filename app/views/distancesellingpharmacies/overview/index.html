Online-only pharmacy

This pharmacy will deliver nationwide. You cannot visit the pharmacy premises to take in or pick up a prescription

This pharmacy offers:

  • NHS one-off prescriptions
  • NHS repeat prescriptions
  • nationwide delivery
  • an app to manage your repeat prescriptions
  • reminders when your repeat prescriptions are due

Online-only pharmacies are regulated by the General Pharmaceutical Council in the same way as high street pharmacies.

Search for this pharmacy's inspection report

Repeat prescriptions

You can choose this pharmacy to have your repeat prescriptions sent to them by your GP surgery. This means that you will not have to visit the surgery every time you need a repeat prescription.

This pharmacy will be able to order repeat prescriptions on your behalf from your GP surgery.

How to use an online-only pharmacy

Delivery

Prescriptions will take up to 4 working days to be delivered after being received by this pharmacy.

Some medicines may need to be ordered in by the pharmacy in which case delivery may take longer.

Contact this pharmacy if you need your medicine urgently.
