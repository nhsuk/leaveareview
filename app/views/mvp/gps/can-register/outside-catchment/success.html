You can register to this GP

Although you may be able to register to this GP surgery, you may not be able to get some services as you’re located out of the catchment area.

Alternatively, you can find another GP surgery using the link below.

Check another postcode

Register online using the NHS website

Registration will be faster and easier if you have your personal information ready.

You do not need proof of address or immigration status, ID or an NHS number to register online with a GP.

What you'll be asked during online registration

You'll be asked:

  • for your name and address
  • for the name and address of the GP you're registered with now, if there is one
  • for your NHS number, if you know it
  • for the name and address of an emergency contact
  • for the name and address of your carer, if you have one
  • about your medical history
  • about allergies you might have
  • about medication you might be taking
  • for a way to contact you, like a phone number or email address

Other ways to register

To find out more:

  • check the GP surgery website
  • phone the GP surgery reception on 020 8659 9343
  • email info@surgery.co.uk
  • visit the GP surgery to collect a form – get directions (opens in Google Maps)
