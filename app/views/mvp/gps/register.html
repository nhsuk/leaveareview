Register with this GP surgery

You can register online with this GP surgery here on the NHS website.

You'll be asked some questions during registration. It will be faster and easier if you have your personal information ready.

What you'll be asked during online registration

You'll be asked for:

  • your name and address
  • name and address of the GP you're registered with now, if there is one
  • your NHS number, if you know it
  • name and address of an emergency contact
  • name and address of your carer, if you have one
  • about your medical history
  • about allergies you might have
  • about medication you might be taking
  • a way to contact you like a phone number or email address

You do not need proof of address or immigration status, ID or an NHS number to register online with a GP.

Register online with this GP

If you register online you'll get all the usual GP services, such as appointments with a nurse or doctor. There are also other ways to register.
