About this department

This A&E is for anyone aged 1 or over.

This Department is managed by Kent Community Health NHS Foundation Trust. They are not the primary provider at this hospital.

An A&E department (also known as emergency department or casualty) deals with genuine life-threatening emergencies, such as:

  • loss of consciousness
  • acute confused state and fits that are not stopping
  • chest pain
  • breathing difficulties
  • severe bleeding that cannot be stopped
  • severe allergic reactions
  • severe burns or scalds
  • stroke
  • major trauma such as a road traffic accident

Less severe injuries can be treated in urgent care centres (also known as minor injuries units and walk-in centres), which can treat minor illnesses without an appointment.
