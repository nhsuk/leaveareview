About this department

This Department is managed by Maidstone and Tunbridge Wells NHS Trust.

Sevenoaks Hospital is a dedicated centre for the diagnosis, management and treatment of adult patients with upper limb, hip and knee and foot and ankle complaints.

The centre has outpatients, theatre services and diagnostics on site as well as associated specialist Physiotherapy and Occupational Therapy. It has been designed to provide excellent, comprehensive and rapid recovery care for patients.

Treatments and services

  • Foot and Ankle
  • Fracture - Non Emergency
  • Hand and Wrist
  • Hip replacement
  • Knee replacement
  • Shoulder and Elbow
