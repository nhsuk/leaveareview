Order a repeat prescription

To use this service, you must:

  • be registered at a GP surgery in England
  • be aged 13 or older
  • already have a repeat prescription approved by your GP

You can use this service to:

  • order a repeat prescription
  • check the status of a prescription you have ordered
  • choose which high street pharmacy you would like to collect from

You cannot use this service to:

  • order a repeat prescription from an online-only pharmacy.
