You are not registered for NHS login

To use the repeat prescription online service you will need to register for a NHS login. This makes it easier for you to use NHS online services.

What you will need

In order to register for an NHS login you will need one of the following at hand:

  • a valid drivers licence
  • a valid passport
  • an EU identity card

You will need to take photos of the chosen ID, of yourself and you will need to upload the images.
