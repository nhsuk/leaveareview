How to prove your identity without uploading a photo of your ID

You can prove your identity by using a 3-stage process:

  • You will first need to sign up for an NHS login with your laptop or desktop - www.nhs.uk/register

  • Once you have an NHS login go to the Repeat Prescription section.

  • Click the link ‘How to prove who you are without sending a photo of your ID’ and follow the instructions.
