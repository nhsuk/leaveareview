Can I use this service?

Not everyone can order repeat prescriptions online.

For example, to use this service you must:

  • already have a repeat prescription approved by your GP
  • be registered at a GP surgery in England
  • be aged 13 or older

We would like to ask you a few questions before taking you through the process of registering for an NHS login.

This could save you time.
