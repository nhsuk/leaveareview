Before you start

To order repeat prescriptions online, you will need an NHS login.

NHS login makes it easier and quicker for you to securely access digital health and care services.

What you will need

In order to register for an NHS login you will need one of the following at hand:

  • a valid drivers licence
  • a valid passport
  • an EU identity card
Information:

You will need to take photos of the chosen ID and of yourself. You will then need to upload them.

Can I use this service?

Not everyone is eligible to order repeat prescriptions online. So we will need to ask you a few questions to check if you are eligible, before taking you through the process of registering for an NHS login.

If you do not want to check your eligibility, you can go ahead and register for an NHS login.
