Registering for an NHS login using a laptop or desktop PC

To register for your NHS login, you will need to prove who you are by uploading photos of an ID document and a video.

This is easier to do with a mobile or tablet device than with a laptop or PC. Because the camera on your laptop or PC is currently not supported to take high quality photos we suggest that you do one of the following:

Option 1

Go to the NHS login registration page: www.nhsuk/register on your mobile or tablet using a browser such as Safari, Chrome, Firefox or Internet Explorer. Proceed with the registration on your device and upload the required photos and video.

Option 2

Register for an NHS login with your laptop or PC - www.nhsuk/register

After registering you will need to prove your identity using a driving license or a passport.

As you will not be able to do this on your device, you will need some registration details from your GP surgery.

You will be given the following details:

  • Linkage Key (could also be called Passphrase)
  • O.D.S. Code (could also be called Organisation Code or Practice I.D.)
  • Account I.D.

When you have all 3 details, go back to www.nhsuk/prescriptions to login to online services and use the 3 registration details to prove who you are.
