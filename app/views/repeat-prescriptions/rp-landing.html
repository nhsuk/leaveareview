Repeat prescription online service

If you're regularly prescribed the same medicine, your GP may offer a prescription that can be repeated several times.

You can use this service to:

  • order a repeat prescription
  • check the status of a prescription you have ordered
  • choose which high street pharmacy you would like to collect from

You cannot use this service:

  • to order a repeat prescription from an online-only pharmacy.
  • to order an appliance from a dispensing appliance contractor.
  • if you have already used another online prescription service.
