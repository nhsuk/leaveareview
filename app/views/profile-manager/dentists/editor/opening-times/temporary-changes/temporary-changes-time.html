Enter opening times in the 24 hour format

  • For midnight use 00:00 and for midday use 12:00
  • Opening times must close at 23:59

Times must not overlap over 2 days. For example, if on Wednesday a pharmacy opens at 7pm and closes at 2am on Thursday morning, enter:

19:00 - 23:59 for Wednesday and then 00:00 - 02:00 in a seperate entry for Thursday
