NHS eye tests

This optician provides NHS eye tests performed by an Optometrist who is trained to recognise eye abnormalities.

Your eye test will include:

  • Tests to focus and coordinate the eyes
  • Examinations and tests to detect conditions such as glaucoma, macular degeneration and cataracts

Find out more about NHS eye tests

Specialist eye care

The following specialist treatments are also provided that may be available on the NHS:

  • Diabetic eye screening to check for eye problems caused by diabetes
  • Screening for minor eye conditions including red eye, floaters & flashing lights, in growing lashes, watery eyes and superficial foreign bodies
  • Glaucoma eye tests including an eye pressure test, an examination of the front part of your eye and a visual field test
  • Domiciliary services for patients that cannot reach an optician

Free NHS eye tests and help with the cost of replacement glasses or contact lenses

Some people are entitled to a free NHS eye test or an optical voucher to help reduce the cost of glasses or contact lenses

You should check if you’re eligible for help to pay your eye care costs. This is because you may be fined up to £100 plus the cost of your treatment if you claim for free treatment you’re not entitled to.

Hearing services

This optician also provides NHS hearing services including:

  • NHS hearing tests
  • NHS hearing aids and fitting

Your GP can refer you to this service.
