General dentistry

This dental practice provides NHS treatments including:

  • Check-ups
  • Fillings
  • Dentures
  • Crowns
  • Bridges

Find out more about NHS dental treatments

Specialist Dental Care

The following specialist treatments are also provided that may be available on the NHS:

  • Orthodontic treatment to improve crooked teeth
  • Gum disease treatment to treat infected gums
  • Root canal treatment to treat the infection at the centre of a tooth
  • Oral surgery including wisdom tooth extraction
  • Sedation services for patients with dental anxiety
  • Domiciliary services for patients that cannot reach a dental surgery

NHS dental costs

How much you have to pay for NHS dental treatment depends on what you’re having done.

Find out how much you have to pay for NHS dental treatment.

Treatments such as veneers and braces are only available on the NHS if there’s a clinical need for them.

Cosmetic treatments, such as teeth whitening, are not available on the NHS.

Ask your dentist whether the treatment they’re recommending is available on the NHS and how much it will cost before you go ahead.
