Find out more about NHS dental treatments

What you can get on the NHS

Check-ups and advice are always available on the NHS. You can get other treatments on the NHS if your dentist says there’s a medical need for them.

How much does NHS dental treatment cost?

You have to pay for all NHS dental care and treatment. How much you pay depends on what you’re having done.

You may be able to get help with dental costs if you need it.
