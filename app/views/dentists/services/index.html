NHS treatments you may be able to get at this dental surgery

  • Check-ups and polishing
  • Crowns, bridges, fillings
  • Advice about looking after your teeth and gums
  • Braces and other orthodontic treatment
  • Gum disease treatment
  • Surgery - including taking teeth out
  • Root canal treatment
  • Medicine to help you relax (sedation)
  • Home visits if needed

Check-ups and advice are always available on the NHS.

You can get other treatments on the NHS if your dentist says there’s a clinical need for them.

Find out more about NHS dental treatments

NHS dental treatment costs

You generally have to pay for NHS dental care and treatment. How much you pay depends on what you’re having done.

NHS dental care is free for children and for people on some benefits.

Find out more about dental costs

You may be able to get help with dental costs if you need it.

You should check what help you could get to pay for NHS costs. This is because you may be fined up to £100 plus the cost of your treatment if you claim for free treatment you’re not entitled to.
