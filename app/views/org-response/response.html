How to respond to reviews

Positive reviews
  1. Address the reviewer with their screen name, or ‘Dear patient’ if anonymous
  2. Thank them for their feedback
  3. Tailor your response to include what’s mentioned in the review
  4. Offer to pass feedback to the staff
  5. Sign off with your name and role
Negative reviews
  1. Address the reviewer with their screen name, or ‘Dear patient’ if anonymous
  2. Thank them for their feedback
  3. Identify yourself and your role
  4. Apologise and empathise for their poor experience
  5. Clarify what happened, if possible
  6. Tailor your response to include what’s mentioned in the review
  7. Offer reassurance and clarify any improvements you intend to make
  8. Tell them about any relevant help, online or offline
  9. Offer a direct telephone number or email address for them to contact you
  10. Sign off with your name and role
