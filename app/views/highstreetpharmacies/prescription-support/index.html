Prescription queries

Pharmacists can answer your questions on prescription and over-the-counter medicines such as paracetamol.

They can also help with the disposal of unwanted or out-of-date medicines.

This pharmacy also provides:

NHS New medicine service

Advice and support service for patients who may find it difficult to understand how and when to take new medicines for:

  • Asthma
  • Chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD)
  • Type 2 diabetes
  • High blood pressure
  • Conditions which need blood thinning medicines

NHS Medicine use review

A face-to-face medicine check-up consultation by a pharmacist for patients who are regularly taking several prescription medicines or have a long-term illness. The service focuses on:

  • Increasing your knowledge of the medicines you take
  • Why you take them
  • How you can take them more easily
