This pharmacy offers these free NHS services to help you with your medicines.

Medicine questions

Pharmacists can answer your questions about prescription medicines and those that you can buy such as paracetamol.

They can also help with the disposal of unwanted or out of date medicines.

Find out about commonly prescribed medicines and medicines you can buy without a prescription in our medicines A-Z.
